Our Mission

The RTG Sanskrit Project is a collaborative initiative between Vyoma Linguistic Labs Foundation and the Aapatsahaaya Foundation, designed to integrate the richness of Sanskrit and traditional Indian knowledge systems into modern education.

We work with underserved communities, providing holistic education that blends heritage with innovation. Our programs include Sanskrit language learning, yoga, meditation, cow awareness, and teacher training — all aimed at empowering students and preserving cultural values for future generations.

"Why is a triangle with two equal sides called an isosceles triangle?"

Teaching it as sama-dvi-bhuja-trikoṇa brings clarity, understanding, and joy to learning — connecting modern education with the precision and beauty of Sanskrit.
