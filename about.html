About the RTG Sanskrit Project

Reviving Traditional Gurukula with Modern Education

Our Vision

The RTG Sanskrit Project is a collaborative initiative between Vyoma Linguistic Labs Foundation and the Aapatsahaaya Foundation, designed to integrate the richness of Sanskrit and traditional Indian knowledge systems into modern education.

We work with underserved communities, providing holistic education that blends heritage with innovation. Our programs include Sanskrit language learning, yoga, meditation, cow awareness, and teacher training — all aimed at empowering students and preserving cultural values for future generations.

Our Journey

1

Phase 1: Planting the Seeds

Established foundational programs in three carefully selected schools, introducing Sanskrit education and traditional knowledge systems to underserved communities.

2

Phase 2: Nurturing Growth

Expanded programs to reach 450+ students, trained 15+ teachers, and implemented sustainable educational frameworks combining heritage with modern pedagogy.

3

Phase 3: Expanding Horizons

Scaling our reach to more schools, digitalizing educational tools, implementing measurement systems, and building a network of trained educators for lasting impact.
